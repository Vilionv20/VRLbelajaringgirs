16 Tenses Lengkap

1. Present Simple

Rumus: Subject + V1 (s/es)

Contoh: She works at a hospital.

Penggunaan: Kebiasaan, fakta umum

2. Present Continuous

Rumus: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

Contoh: They are playing football now.

Penggunaan: Aktivitas sedang berlangsung

3. Present Perfect

Rumus: Subject + have/has + V3

Contoh: I have finished my homework.

Penggunaan: Pengalaman, hasil aktivitas

16. Future Perfect Continuous

Rumus: Subject + will have been + V-ing

Contoh: By next year, I will have been studying English for 5 years.

Penggunaan: Durasi aktivitas di masa depan
