Complete Grammar Guide

1. Present Simple

Formula: Subject + V1 (s/es)

Example: "She works at a bank."

Usage: Habits, general truths

2. Present Continuous

Formula: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

Example: "They are playing football."

Usage: Actions happening now

16. Future Perfect Continuous

Formula: Subject + will have been + V-ing

Example: "By 2025, I will have been studying English for 10 years."

Usage: Duration before a future time

Parts of Speech

Nouns: Person, place, thing (book, teacher)

Verbs: Action words (run, eat)
