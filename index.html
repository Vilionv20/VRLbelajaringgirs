16 Tenses Mastery

1. Present Simple

Rumus: Subject + V1 (s/es)

Contoh: She works at a hospital.

Penggunaan: Kebiasaan, fakta umum

2. Present Continuous

Rumus: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

Contoh: They are playing football.

Penggunaan: Aktivitas sedang berlangsung

Percakapan Sehari-hari

Di Restoran

A: Can I get a menu, please?
B: Here you go. Are you ready to order?

Di Bandara

A: Where is the boarding gate for flight 707?
B: It's at gate C12, just follow the signs.
